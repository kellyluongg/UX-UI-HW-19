The New Mom Project


Revamping the website of a non-profit organization to enhance user engagement and improve the overall user experience. The goal is to optimize the user interface, making it more user-friendly for individuals who may not be proficient with technology, thus ensuring that they can easily access the assistance they require through the website.



UX/UI Web Design • Mockup and Wireframes • User Testing

The Problem


New mothers seek help through online platforms and are occasionally in need of help for basic necessities, support for postpartum depression, anxiety, and feeling overwhelmed. Many online platforms offer resources and support to help financially or mentally.



How can we provide an easily accessible platform for struggling new mothers to their needs of basic necessities and console their stress of being a new mother?
